## Mission
To create a fairer travel ecosystem where hosts keep 100% of every booking
and travellers can trust exactly what they are booking.

## Core Value Proposition
- Zero commission — hosts pay a flat monthly fee only
- Properties, activities, car rentals, dining and safaris on one platform
- AI-powered travel assistant (Aria) available 24/7
- TripVerify: video-verified property badges
- SpaceChat: built-in host-traveller messaging
- TripGuard: free AI vehicle damage assessment
- DeviceGuard: optional GPS phone tracking

## Pricing
- Standard listing: R450/month (~$24 USD) — zero commission
- Hotels/resorts: R450 per 5 rooms/month
- Annual: 12 months + 1 free
- TripVerify: R200/month
- DeviceGuard: R50/month
- TripAssist (Aria): R50/month, 7-day free trial
